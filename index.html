Guru Nanak Institutions Of Teachnology

Guru Nanak Institute of Technology established in 2008, assures world class technical education and an appropriate career to every student offering extensive exposure to the latest market trends in technical field.

Spacious and green, the ambience of campus makes learning a cherished experience for students as well as teachers. Well-furnished lecture halls, air conditioned digital library with latest books, journals and e-books, book bank facility, modern science and technical labs equipped with latest technology, smart computer labs powered by Dell and HP systems of latest configuration, college canteen with modern furniture as well as hygienic food and Wi-Fi campus provide an environment that fosters the growth of learning and self-knowledge.

GNIT leaves no stone unturned to equip the students with the right set of skills and values through a well-structured curriculum including industrial visits, guest lectures, personality development program, sports and games and co-curricular activities and value addition modules on emotional intelligence, team building, business etiquettes and many more. The institute upholds high standards of instruction where emphasis is on the development of analytical ability and creative thinking employing discussion, presentations, seminars, case study method and on cultivating the research talent of the students.

Well qualified, competent and compassionate faculty with ideas, creativity and conviction, in each stream provide strong academic background to the students along with fostering the talent and creating budding business navigators. With focus on quality technical education the Institute offers B. Tech. in the branches of Computer Science & Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Civil Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. It also offers M.Tech. Programme in Computer Science and Engineering and Electronics & Communication Engineering.

Guru Nanak Institutions Of Managment

Guru Nanak Institute of Management (GNIM), Mullana,established in 2008, is creating new dimensions in academic excellence, executive development and industry interface. The institute conducts two year, full time M.B.A. programme affiliated to Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra and equips the students with managerial and entrepreneurial skills along with the knowledge of business operations.

Guru Nanak Institute of Management aims at growth of urban and rural youth. GNIM is providing career-oriented education to the students to furnish them with requisite qualification and training. The institute is fully conscious of the needs and aspirations of the student-community and keeps itself abreast of the changes taking place on the industrial front of the country.

GNIM has excellent infrastructure and facilities like a Centralized Air-Conditioned Library with latest books, journals, magazines and e-books, membership of On-line Libraries; state-of-the-art Computing facilities; smart Air-Conditioned Lecture Halls with audio-visual teaching aids such as data projector, E-pen etc.; Air-conditioned Conference Halls; Air-cooled Hostels with all the modern amenities; cultural, recreational and sports facilities. Above all students are motivated by academic environment which combines scholastic rigor with a high level of industry interaction.

The courses offered are geared towards preparing the students to meet the challenges in the highly competitive and technologically advanced business environment of today. MBA programme has an intake of 120 students pursuing one of the following combinations viz. Marketing & Finance; Marketing & Human Resource; Marketing &Information Technology; Finance & Information Technology; Human Recourse & Information Technology plus Human Resource & Finance.

Shri Akhand Path Sahib

GNI Mullana ambience was completely filled with spiritual fervour as Shri Akhand Path Sahib was organised to celebrate the Guru Nanak Dev Ji Prakash Utsav. The students and faculty members offered their voluntary services to complement the pious occasion. The completion of AkhandPath was followed by Kirtan, Ardas and GuruKaLanger.

The students were apprised of the teachings of the great saints and Gurus. GNI family believes in imparting great values and principles to its students by organising such religious functions annually. The whole management of GNI Honourable Chairman Mr. Tarlochan Singh, Reverend General Secretary Mr. Harjinder Singh and Dean of Colleges Dr. Sachin Chawla appreciated the efforts put in by the faculty and students for the smooth functioning of the programme.

They also motivated and inspired the students to follow the foosteps of these great soul to make their life successful
